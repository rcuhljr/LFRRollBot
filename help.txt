L5R Dice Roller 
Examples: 'r 5k3' rolls 5 d10 and keeps the best 3, exploding on 10s. 
'r 5ke3' rerolls 1's one time(emphasis). 'r 3ku3' this roll has no explosions(unskilled). 'r 5km3' exploding on 9s. 
r/roll can be dropped if dicesuke isn't in the channel. /invite to bring the bot to a new channel if you have op, or message
the bot with @join #channel if you don't have op. The BNF that this bot follows is at found at
